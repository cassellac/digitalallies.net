Logo Usage Guidelines

  • Maintain minimum clear space of 1x logo height on all sides
  • Never stretch, skew, or alter proportions
  • Use on backgrounds with sufficient contrast
  • Provide alternative versions for dark backgrounds
  • Always use provided vector files for print and high-resolution displays

Color Usage Guidelines

  • All color combinations meet WCAG AA contrast standards (4.5:1 minimum)
  • Never rely solely on color to convey information
  • Use primary blue for interactive elements and focus states
  • Green indicates success, progress, or positive actions
  • Neutrals provide hierarchy and readability
